Tiffany & Co. Unveils Its New T1 Legacy Collection

The latest addition to the Tiffany T collection reinvents an iconic symbol of the storied fine jewelry house. Get a first look at the new Tiffany T1 collection here.

  • by Alyssa Montemurro
  • Last Updated:  May 12, 2020

While the novel coronavirus outbreak may have put a damper on some of the seasons most anticipated events and releases, including this months annual Met Gala and the upcoming Paris haute couture shows, it isnt stopping Tiffany & Co. from pushing forward with the launch of its new Tiffany T1 collection.

Debuting this month, the new Tiffany T1 collection pays homage to the brands rich historyby reinterpreting its famous T motifan inspiration behind the brands jewelry designs since the 1980sacross a curated edit of nine stunning new pieces. Designed by Reed Krakoff, Tiffanys chief artistic officer, the Tiffany T1 collection includes a range of 18-karat rose gold bracelets and rings in varying widths, as well as five unique designs featuring pav diamonds hand-set by artisans in a honeycomb pattern for maximum radiance.

At Tiffany, we believe that luxury should be effortless and irreverent, says Krakoff, While designing Tiffany T1, we wanted to honor the legacy of the iconic T motif, but also elevate and modernize it with hand-set diamonds in the beveled edges. Through these pieces, we convey that precious stones arent just for special occasionsthey can be worn every day as a celebration of yourself.

Designed for women with a point of view, the Tiffany T1 collection is intended to offer a modern and effortless new way to wear fine jewelry, with its elegantly angular and feminine proportions creating a bold yet refined statement for the wearer. Though envisioned as a tribute to the houses storied past, the new T motif also taps into a more deeply personal sentiment by serving as a symbol of individual strength and self empowerment courtesy of the unbroken circle that is formed through its unique design.

In addition to the key pieces that are intended to be worn every day and for all occasions, the Tiffany T1 collection is also marked by the debut of a striking and exclusive new high jewelry necklace featuring more than 240 mixed-cut diamonds and weighing a total of 14 carats. Additional Tiffany T1 designs in 18-karat yellow and white gold metalways, in addition to bracelets, rings, earrings and pendants set with baguette diamonds, will also become available later this fall.

The new Tiffany T1 collection pieces range in price from $850 to $28,000, with the high jewelry necklace priced at $150,000. For more information, please visit https://www.tiffany.com/.
